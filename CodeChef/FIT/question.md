## Problem Statement

### Fitness

Chef wants to become fit for which he decided to walk to the office and return home by walking. It is known that Chef's office is XXX km away from his home.

If his office is open on 555 days in a week, find the number of kilometers Chef travels through office trips in a week.

### Input Format

- First line will contain TTT, number of test cases. Then the test cases follow.

- Each test case contains of a single line consisting of single integer XXX.

### Output Format

For each test case, output the number of kilometers Chef travels through office trips in a week.

### Constraints

- 1≤T≤101 \leq T \leq 101≤T≤10

- 1≤X≤101 \leq X \leq 101≤X≤10

### Sample 1:

Input

Output

```

100
```

### Explanation:

**Test case 111:** The office is 111 km away. Thus, to go to the office and come back home, Chef has to walk 222 kms in a day. In a week with 555 working days, Chef has to travel 2⋅5=102\cdot 5 = 102⋅5=10 kms in total.

**Test case 222:** The office is 333 kms away. Thus, to go to the office and come back home, Chef has to walk 666 kms in a day. In a week with 555 working days, Chef has to travel 6⋅5=306\cdot 5 = 306⋅5=30 kms in total.

**Test case 333:** The office is 777 kms away. Thus, to go to the office and come back home, Chef has to walk 141414 kms in a day. In a week with 555 working days, Chef has to travel 14⋅5=7014\cdot 5 = 7014⋅5=70 kms in total.

**Test case 444:** The office is 101010 kms away. Thus, to go to the office and come back home, Chef has to walk 202020 kms in a day. In a week with 555 working days, Chef has to travel 20⋅5=10020\cdot 5 = 10020⋅5=100 kms in total.
